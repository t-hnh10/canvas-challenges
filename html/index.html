Kazka

Introduction

Kazka (stylized as KAZKA; from Ukrainian казка (kazka), "fairy tale") is a band that performs pop with elements of electro-folk. Since its creation in 2017, vocalist Oleksandra Zaritska, sopilka player Dmytro Mazuriak and multi-instrumentalist Mykyta Budash have become a Ukrainian sensation, "breakthrough of the year"; the first Ukrainian-speaking band make the world famous chart SHAZAM; and a record holder among Ukrainian artists on YouTube. The band has topped the charts in a dozen countries, including Ukraine, Latvia, Bulgaria, Armenia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Belarus, Russia, Colombia, and so on.
